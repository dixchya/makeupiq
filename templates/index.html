How it works
Four simple steps to your perfect match
📷
Step 1 — Upload
Take or upload a clear, front-facing photo in natural daylight. Avoid heavy filters for best results.
🔬
Step 2 — Analyse
Our AI algorithm uses K-Means colour clustering to detect your skin tone depth and undertone from your image.
💄
Step 3 — Match
Products from real brands are matched to your exact shade. Filter by budget and skin type.
✨
Step 4 — Style
Get a personalised makeup colour guide — lip shades, eyeshadow, blush and highlighter — all tailored to your undertone.
About the technology
MakeupIQ uses a K-Means machine learning algorithm to analyse your skin tone locally on our server — your image is never shared with third parties. You can delete your image at any time from the Privacy screen.
